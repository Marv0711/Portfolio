About me
Hello, I am a Frontend Developer based in Dresden, Germany. Originally coming from the IT field, I made the transition to software development with the help of a bootcamp, discovering my passion for crafting engaging and user-friendly user interfaces along the way.
When I'm not in front of a screen, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends. Shared laughter and the creation of lasting memories are precious moments for me. Life is made up of a fascinating blend of digital pixels and personal connections. I look forward to incorporating this perspective into my work.
At the same time, I appreciate the simplicity of everyday life and the ability to overcome complex problems through thoughtful thinking. In the world of zeros and ones, I seek elegant solutions that are not only functional but also tailored to the end user.Part-time, I am studying Cyber Security on the IU. The constantly advancing nature of the IT landscape fascinates me, and I am enthusiastic about deepening my knowledge to contribute to the security of future digital spaces.
